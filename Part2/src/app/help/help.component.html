Help & Troubleshooting

How do I add an item?

Go to the Inventory page, fill in all required fields (ID, Name, Supplier, Quantity, Price, etc.), and click Add Item. The ID must be unique.

How do I update an item?

In the Inventory page, enter the current item name in the "Item Name" field, modify any other fields, then click Update Item.

How do I delete an item?

In the Inventory page, enter the exact item name in the "Delete by item name" field and click Delete Item. A confirmation dialog will appear.

Why can't I find an item in search?

Search is case-insensitive and matches partial names. Ensure you are on the Search page and the item exists in the current session.

What does "Popular Item" mean?

Popular items are marked with "Yes" in the Popular field. You can view only popular items by clicking Show Popular Items on the Inventory page.

Is my data saved after closing the browser?

No. This application is session-based only. All data is lost when the browser tab is closed.
